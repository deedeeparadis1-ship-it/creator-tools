Set up Stripe in about 15 minutes.

Stripe is the service that takes your buyer's card and puts the money in your bank. It's free to set up, millions of businesses use it, and you only do this once. Here's exactly what to expect so none of it catches you off guard.

Before you start

Have these ready first.

Gather these three things and the setup goes quick. Stripe will ask for them as you go.

Have ready

Your basics

This is all it takes to create the account itself.

  • An email you actually check
  • A password
  • Your country
Have ready

Your payout details

Where Stripe sends your money, plus a few details to verify you.

  • Bank account and routing number
  • Your legal name and address
  • Your date of birth
Have ready

Your ID

Stripe confirms you're really you. Quick, and it keeps your account secure.

  • A government photo ID
  • Last 4 of your SSN (in the US)
  • Takes about a minute
Step by step

The whole setup, start to finish.

Six steps, in order. Take them one at a time and you'll come out with a working Stripe account.

★
By the end

A Stripe account ready to connect to Systeme.

That's the finish line. Here's how you get there.

1

Create your account

Go to stripe.com and click Sign up. Enter your email, your name, and a password.

2

Verify your email

Stripe sends you a confirmation link. Click it to confirm your address.

3

Tell Stripe about you

Choose individual (totally fine to start), then add your name, address, and a short line on what you sell, like "digital guides."

4

Add your bank account

This is where your money lands. Enter your account and routing numbers.

5

Verify your identity

Upload a photo ID and, in the US, the last four of your SSN. This keeps your account secure.

6

You're live

Your account is ready. Now connect it to whatever platform you use to sell.

Good to know

A few things that trip people up.

None are a big deal. Knowing them ahead just saves a little stress.

Don't worry

Individual is fine

You don't need a registered business or an LLC to start. Choose individual or sole proprietor and you're good.

  • No LLC required
  • Individual works great
  • You can upgrade later
Don't worry

First payout is slower

Your very first sale may take a few business days to reach your bank while Stripe gets set up. After that it's quick and automatic.

  • First one takes a few days
  • Then it's automatic
  • Completely normal
Handy

Practice with test mode

Stripe has a test mode so you can run a pretend sale with no real money. Perfect for checking your setup before you go live.

  • Practice safely
  • No real charges
  • Then flip to live
Connect it up

Last step: link it up.

Here's the good news. Stripe connects to almost every major platform that takes payments, so once it's set up, you just link it to whatever you use to sell.

How it works

Connect once, use forever

In whatever platform you sell on, find the payment settings and connect Stripe. A couple of clicks and they're linked. Every product you make from then on uses it automatically.

  • Find payment settings
  • Connect Stripe, one time
  • Works for every future product
You're not locked in

Works with most platforms

Stripe plugs into almost every major tool that takes payments, so you can use whatever fits you best, now or later.

  • Systeme, Kajabi, Podia, Stan
  • Shopify, Thinkific, and more
  • Use what works for you
