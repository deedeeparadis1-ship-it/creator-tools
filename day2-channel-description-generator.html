Before you start
Where does this go?
Once you have your description, here is exactly where to paste it in YouTube:
1
Go to studio.youtube.com
2
Click Customization in the left menu
3
Click the Basic Info tab
4
Paste your description into the Description field
5
Click Publish in the top right corner
