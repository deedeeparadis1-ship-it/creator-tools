Your [product name] is on its way to your inbox right now. If you don't see it in a few minutes, check your spam or promotions folder, and add me to your contacts so we stay connected.

Here's what I'd do next: [one simple first step with the product].

And if you want to keep going with people who are right where you are, come hang out in my free community. [community link]

So glad you're here.
