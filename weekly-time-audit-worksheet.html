7-Day YouTube Challenge — Bonus Resource

Find your content hours.

A one-week time audit to discover where your time is actually going — and carve out a realistic content schedule that fits your real life.

How to use this worksheet

Step 1
Fill in what you actually do in each time block — be honest, not aspirational
Step 2
At the end of each day, highlight blocks where you had free or unstructured time
Step 3
After 7 days, answer the reflection questions to spot your hidden pockets
Step 4
Build your content schedule — just 4 small blocks per week is all you need
