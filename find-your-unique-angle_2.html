Find Your
Unique Angle

Stop saying what everyone else is saying. This worksheet helps you find the video idea that only YOU can make.

The creators who grow aren't always the most polished or the most consistent. They're the ones brave enough to say something different -- something that comes from their own experience, their own story, their own worldview.

You have that. Work through each step below. At every step you'll find a ready-made AI prompt you can copy and paste into ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI tool you already use. It'll help you generate ideas so you're never staring at a blank box wondering what to write.

1

What does everyone in your niche keep saying?

Start by listening. What's the advice that gets repeated over and over in your space? The "wisdom" everyone seems to agree on? Write down 3-5 things you hear constantly. These are your windows -- and one of them might be ready for a brick.

Example: "Just be consistent and you'll grow." / "You need to go viral to get subscribers."
AI Prompt to copy USE IN CHATGPT OR CLAUDE
I make YouTube videos about [your niche]. List the most common advice people give in this space that gets repeated over and over. Then tell me which ones might be worth challenging with a contrarian take.
Then paste it into ChatGPT or Claude
2

What have YOU tried that didn't work the way they said?

This is where your real story lives. Think about the advice from Step 1. Did any of it fall flat for you? Did you follow the rules and still not get the result? That gap between what they promised and what you experienced is gold. You're not complaining -- you're being honest. And your audience needs that honesty.

Example: "I posted consistently for 6 months and barely grew. Consistency alone wasn't the answer."
AI Prompt to copy USE IN CHATGPT OR CLAUDE
I tried [what you tried] in my YouTube channel about [your niche] and it didn't work the way everyone said it would. Help me turn that experience into a contrarian video angle my audience would find interesting and relatable.
Then paste it into ChatGPT or Claude
3

What have you figured out that surprised even you?

Now flip it. What DID work -- especially if it was unexpected? What did you discover by actually doing the thing, not just reading about it? The best contrarian takes aren't manufactured. They come from your own experience. Think about a result you got that made you go, "Wait -- that actually worked?"

Example: "I made money on YouTube before I even hit 1,000 subscribers -- which everyone said was impossible."
AI Prompt to copy USE IN CHATGPT OR CLAUDE
Here's something that surprised me in my own YouTube journey: [what surprised you]. My channel is about [your niche] and my audience is [who you serve]. Help me figure out if this could be a strong contrarian video idea, what the hook might be, and why people would click on it.
Then paste it into ChatGPT or Claude
4

Build your video arc

Every great contrarian video follows the same simple structure: Belief, Crack, Story, New View. Start with what your viewer already believes. Show them where it breaks down. Use your own story as the proof. Then give them a new way to see it. Fill in the boxes below -- then use the AI prompt to help flesh it out.

Your video arc

Belief -- what they think is true
Crack -- where it breaks down
Story -- your real experience
New View -- the better way
AI Prompt to copy USE IN CHATGPT OR CLAUDE
Help me build a YouTube video arc using this structure: Belief, Crack, Story, New View. My video topic is [your topic]. My audience is [who you serve]. The common belief I want to challenge is [the belief]. My personal experience is [your story]. Fill in each section with specific, engaging suggestions.
Then paste it into ChatGPT or Claude
5

Turn it into a title and thumbnail

Your title should create a little tension. Make the viewer think, "Wait -- really? That can't be right." Then they click to find out. Keep your title under 55 characters. Your thumbnail text should be 1-3 words that tease without giving it all away. You're not clickbaiting -- you're opening a loop.

Keep it under 55 characters
Keep it under 55 characters
AI Prompt to copy USE IN CHATGPT OR CLAUDE
I'm making a YouTube video for an audience of [who you serve]. The video topic is [your topic] and my contrarian angle is [your angle]. Give me 5 title options that are each under 55 characters, create curiosity or tension, and match my contrarian angle. Also give me 5 thumbnail text options of 1-3 words each that tease without giving the answer away.
Then paste it into ChatGPT or Claude

Quick reminder: your thumbnail text should create curiosity or tension -- not give the answer away. The title does the explaining. The thumbnail makes them feel something and want to click.
