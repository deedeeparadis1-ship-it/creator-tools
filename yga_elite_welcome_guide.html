The 8-Week Roadmap
A guide, not a
rigid schedule

This roadmap keeps us moving forward with purpose. But it's flexible -- if something needs attention before we planned for it, just let me know ahead of our call so I can prepare. Your progress drives the schedule, not the other way around.

Phase 1 -- Foundation
1
Clarity and Channel Setup
Your message, your audience, your foundation
▼
  • Define your message, your audience, and what success looks like for you
  • Audit your channel together -- banner, about section, profile photo, everything a new viewer sees first
  • Set a realistic posting goal and talk through what consistency looks like for you
  • Review any content you've already created so nothing goes to waste
2
Content Strategy and Filming
What to talk about and how to show up on camera
▼
  • Build your content plan -- what topics, what format, how long
  • Work on camera comfort and natural delivery
  • Talk through your filming setup and answer any outstanding tech questions
Phase 2 -- Skills Building
3
Editing and Publishing
Getting comfortable with "good enough"
▼
  • By week 3 you should have your first video -- or a few -- recorded and edited. We'll review them together and I'll tell you exactly what to fix and what to leave alone
  • Learn the 80/20 rule of editing -- what viewers actually care about and what they don't
  • Walk through how to upload properly -- title, description, tags, end screens, everything
4
Thumbnails
What makes people click -- and how to create it
▼
  • Learn what makes a thumbnail work -- font, color, photo, text
  • Walk through Canva basics and build your first thumbnails together
  • Feedback on your thumbnails via Loom as needed
5
Editing Confidence and Content Flow
Building your rhythm
▼
  • Review your editing habits -- is your confidence growing?
  • Check in on your content flow -- are you posting consistently?
  • Make sure you're building a real, sustainable posting rhythm
6
Thumbnails and Branding
Making your channel look like you
▼
  • Review your thumbnails -- are they consistent and clickable?
  • Talk through your channel branding -- does it reflect who you are?
  • By now you should feel confident creating thumbnails independently
Phase 3 -- Momentum
7
Audience and Income
Planting the seeds for monetization
▼
  • Look at your analytics together -- what's getting clicks, what's not
  • Talk through YouTube Partner Program requirements and what you're working toward
  • Explore monetization options that fit your niche -- memberships, Super Thanks, and more
  • Talk about how to engage your viewers and build a real community
Phase 4 -- Review and Decision
8
Full Channel Audit
Celebrate, evaluate, decide what's next
▼
  • Step back and look at everything -- thumbnails, videos, analytics, posting habits, growth
  • Celebrate how far you've come -- eight weeks ago you were just getting started
  • Look honestly at what still needs work and what you've got on your own
  • Decide together whether you want to continue with ongoing bi-weekly support at $250/month
