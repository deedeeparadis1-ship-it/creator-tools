Before you start

Have these ready first.

Three things, and you're ready to move fast through this one.

Have ready

Your file

The actual PDF, guide, or download buyers will receive. If what you're selling lives at a link instead of a file, that's fine too, you'll use Redirect to URL instead.

  • A finished PDF or file
  • Or a link, if that's your format
Have ready

Your product details

The name and price you've already decided on. You'll type these in as you build the product.

  • Product name
  • Your price
Have ready

A place to save links

Somewhere you keep track of every link you create, a doc, a notes app, anywhere. You'll need it in the last step.

  • One spot for all your links
  • So nothing gets lost
Step by step

From file to live link.

Six steps, in order. Follow along inside your own Stan account as you go.

★
By the end

A published product with one live link, ready to share.

That's the finish line. Here's how you get there.

1

Upload your file

Scroll down to the file section and upload your PDF. Selling something that lives at a link instead? Choose Redirect to URL and paste it in there instead.

2

Go to Options

This tab is where your delivery and confirmation email live.

3

Turn on your confirmation email

Click Create next to Confirmation Email. This is what sends the moment someone buys.

4

Personalize it

Leave the purple placeholder alone, that's where their name fills in automatically. Update the subject line to something like "Your order from [your name]." Your file goes out right in that email.

5

Hit Publish

That's what takes your product live.

6

Find it and copy your link

Scroll down to My Store, find your product by name, and copy its URL. That one link is what you'll share everywhere.

Good to know

One honest heads-up.

Nothing here will trip you up, but worth knowing going in.

Save it somewhere safe

Don't lose your link

The second you copy that URL, drop it into whatever doc or notes app you use to keep track of your links. You don't want to go hunting for it later.

  • Copy it immediately
  • One safe place for all your links
Worth knowing

Email stays simple here

Stan can send your confirmation email and a couple of follow-ups, but it's not built for full automated sequences. That's exactly fine for getting your first product out the door. It's just something to know if you outgrow it down the road.

  • Confirmation email, covered
  • A couple of extra emails, fine
  • Big automated sequences, not its strength
Get your link out there

One link. Everywhere.

This is the whole reason Stan feels fast, once you have that link, there's nowhere it doesn't work.

Put it

Anywhere people already are

Your Instagram bio, a post caption, your YouTube description, anywhere someone might be reading and ready to click.

  • Instagram bio and posts
  • YouTube description
  • Anywhere you've got an audience
Why it works

No extra steps for them

They click, they land on your checkout, they buy. No funnel to wander through first. That's what makes Stan feel quick for a first product.

  • Click, land, buy
  • Nothing extra in the way
