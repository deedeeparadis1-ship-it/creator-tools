The 8-Week Roadmap
A guide, not a
rigid schedule

This roadmap keeps us moving forward with purpose. But it's flexible -- if something needs attention before we planned for it, just let me know ahead of our call so I can prepare. Your progress drives the schedule, not the other way around.

Phase 1 -- Foundation
1
Clarity and Channel Setup
Your message, your audience, your foundation
▼
  • Define your message, your audience, and what success looks like for you
  • Audit your channel together -- banner, about section, profile photo, everything a new viewer sees first
  • Talk about why you keep deleting your videos and change that habit for good
  • Set a realistic posting goal -- 1 video per week to start
  • Review any footage you've already recorded so nothing goes to waste
2
Content Strategy and Filming
What to talk about and how to show up on camera
▼
  • Build your content plan -- what topics, what format, how long
  • Work on camera comfort and natural delivery
  • Talk through filming outside at community events -- phone setup, audio, what to capture
  • Answer any outstanding tech questions
Phase 2 -- Skills Building
3
Editing and Publishing
Getting comfortable with "good enough"
▼
  • By week 3 you should have your first video -- or 3 or 4 -- recorded and edited. We'll review them together and I'll tell you exactly what to fix and what to leave alone
  • Learn the 80/20 rule of editing -- what viewers actually care about and what they don't
  • Walk through how to upload properly -- title, description, tags, end screens, everything
4
Thumbnails, Part 1 (Done for You)
Your first thumbnails, built together
▼
  • Dee Dee creates your first thumbnails and walks you through every decision -- font, color, photo, text
  • You learn by watching, then you try one yourself
  • Feedback on your attempt delivered via Loom
  • You'll need to send good photos of yourself -- we'll talk about what works best
5
Editing Confidence and Content Flow
Building your rhythm
▼
  • Review your editing habits -- are you improving? Are you still over-cutting?
  • Check in on your community footage -- are you getting what you need out in the field?
  • Make sure you're building a real, sustainable posting rhythm
6
Thumbnails, Part 2 (Done for You)
Handing the wheel over to you
▼
  • Round two of done-for-you thumbnails with more of the process handed off to you
  • By the end of this session you should feel confident creating your own
  • Going forward Dee Dee will review and give feedback -- but you'll be driving
Phase 3 -- Momentum
7
Audience and Income
Planting the seeds for monetization
▼
  • Look at your analytics together -- what's getting clicks, what's not
  • Talk through YouTube Partner Program requirements and what you're working toward
  • Explore monetization options that fit your mission -- memberships, Super Thanks, and more
  • Talk about how to engage your viewers and build a real community around your message
Phase 4 -- Review and Decision
8
Full Channel Audit
Celebrate, evaluate, decide what's next
▼
  • Step back and look at everything -- thumbnails, videos, analytics, posting habits, growth
  • Celebrate how far you've come -- eight weeks ago you had zero published videos
  • Look honestly at what still needs work and what you've got on your own
  • Decide together whether you want to continue with ongoing monthly support
